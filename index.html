CoffeeCore – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 08/07/2025

CoffeeCore respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (“CoffeeCore” or “the App”) designed for coffee farmers.

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a) Personal Information
  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Location (e.g., GPS or coarse location, if enabled)
b) Usage Data
  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Log data (app usage activity, crash reports, diagnostics)
c) Farm-related Data (User Input)
  • Farm size
  • Coffee production volumes
  • Farming practices
  • Harvest and sales records

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the CoffeeCore app
  • Personalize content and user experience
  • Analyze app usage for product development
  • Send important notifications (e.g., updates, alerts)
  • Respond to support inquiries and feedback

3. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

We may share your data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under strict confidentiality
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies when required by law
  • Partners, only with your explicit consent

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

5. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access or correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at infojvalmacis@gmail.com.

CoffeeCore is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
